about me

I'm Daryl Perry, a senior journalism major and creative writing minor from Silver Spring, Maryland. I've written about culture, music, entertainment, and the intersections between fashion and technology. My favorite stories are ones that center human interest.

I'm currently a staff photographer for The Diamondback, UMD's independent newspaper, and on the Press Committee for WMUC 90.5FM, the university's freeform radio station. Outside of student publications, I'm a Hi-Res intern for The Daily Dot.

I aspire to be a multimedia or investigative journalist. Outside of journalism, I enjoy video editing, writing poetry, fiber arts and spaans en nederlands spreken (speaking Spanish and Dutch)!
